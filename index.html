About me

I am a student at the University of Lethbridge graduating next year with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I aim to strengthen my candidacy by demonstrating both my experience developing applications and my capacity for growth.

I enjoy learning about how software development is done in the workplace, and am getting a taste of it by developing full stack webapps with some popular frameworks. Some other areas I enjoy are computer graphics, networking, and functional programming.

I recognize that my value as a junior developer comes from my ability to improve my skills and grow and adapt on the fly. It is my goal to take ownership of this investment and develop the capabilities to add meaningful value to a workplace.

What I'm working on

Stay tuned to see further results on a 2D platformer made with Love2D and Lua (medium term), and a custom game engine written with C and the Vulkan graphics API (long term).
